Privacy Policy - Twitch FlowSwitch

Effective Date: October 2025

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations are performed locally within your browser.

Data Handling: The extension only interacts with Twitch’s video player elements to switch between streams during paid presentations. No external servers are contacted.

Analytics: No tracking, telemetry, or analytics tools are used.

Permissions: The tabs permission is used solely to identify the active Twitch tab so that the popup interface can display the player’s status.

Third Parties: This extension is not affiliated with or endorsed by Twitch Interactive, Inc.
